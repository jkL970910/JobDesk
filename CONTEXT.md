# JobDesk Context

## Glossary

**Source Material** - Raw user input such as resumes, work notes, project summaries, JD gap notes, and guided answers. Source Material can inform extraction and review, but it is not resume-usable evidence by itself.

**Work Experience** - A role-level container for employer, title, team, location, dates, scope, linked Story Targets, and linked Evidence Claims. Reviewing a Work Experience confirms the role container only; it does not approve evidence for resume use.

**Story Target** - A reusable story container that can become resume bullets or Interview Stories after it has enough context and linked evidence. Story Targets include Work Initiatives and Portfolio Projects.

**Work Initiative** - A Story Target under a Work Experience, representing a project, achievement, responsibility, or accomplishment from that role.

**Portfolio Project** - A standalone Story Target outside employment, such as academic, personal, open-source, freelance, or hackathon work.

**Evidence Claim** - The smallest source-backed factual proof unit. Evidence Claims are the only unit that can be approved for resume use.

**Interview Story** - A derived STAR-style output generated from a ready Story Target and approved Evidence Claims. Interview Stories are not a source of truth.

**Library** - The Evidence Library mode for browsing and maintaining canonical assets: Work Experiences, Story Targets, Evidence Claims, and Interview Stories.

**Work Queue** - The action-oriented mode for unresolved work on Library assets, such as import review, Work Experience review, Story Target strengthening, evidence linking, evidence strengthening, evidence approval, and cleanup.
